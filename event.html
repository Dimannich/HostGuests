Описание

Welcome for a true GREEK FEAST! We will make you discover the best of Greek gastronomy. We live in Colonus, a historic district of Athens, very close to the center. On a really big balcony with a panoramic view of the town, come to taste the most famous traditional Greek dishes & wines and enjoy the amazing 180° view of Athens (Acropolis, Mount Lycabettus, Hill of Hippio Colonus). It is very accessible via public transport. Some information about the area: In Colonus, a historic district of Athens, there is the hill of Hippio Colonus, which is besides an important green lung, the connection of the ancient with the modern capital. In Hippio Colonus there was the mosque of Erinous, the tomb of Oedipus, as well as the hero of Theseus, Pirithos and Adrastos. Nowadays you can see the marble plaque of Sophocles, the monuments of archeologists Charles Lenormant and Karl Otfried Muller and the Colonus Theater. Near the Hill of Colonus is the Plato's Academy, a big park where the great philosopher taught.
